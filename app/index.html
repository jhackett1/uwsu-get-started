Get started with your SU

You did it! You have landed at the start of an exciting journey with the University of Westminster Students’ Union.

What is a students’ union? We're a student-led organisation whose main goal is to make sure you are supported throughout your student experience, from academia to your social life.

Join a society

Meet like-minded people and grow your interests.

If there’s one way to make the most of university life it’s by joining a society – or many of them! Societies are the driving force of all the fun opportunities and activities that happen on campus.

Play a sport

Train, get fit, make memories, and have fun while you’re at it.

Our Dragons Sports Clubs are growing in popularity and success every year. Our teams include the British Universities and Colleges Sports (BUCS), as well as recreational sports.

There's an event for you

Whether you like to club, network, or visit a London attraction, there is always something for you.

Regular events include a weekly Monday Nights Madness evening at The Loft student bar, as well as Let’s Go adventures around London.

Plus our upcoming Arrivals Week is not far away – and this isn’t your usual freshers.

Grow professionally

Gain new employable skills, build experience and be a leader.

If you’re motivated to broaden your horizons, we’ve got opportunities for you. You’ll be able to stand as a student officer next year to influence the SU’s direction, and apply for part-time jobs with us.

Get advice

Get the support and guidance you need throughout your studies.

Whether it’s to do with academic issues, or general health and wellbeing, our advisors are here to help.
